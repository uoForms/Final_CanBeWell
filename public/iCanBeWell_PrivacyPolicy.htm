Privacy Policy

 

We developed icanbewell.ca to bring you information about preventing diseases or to enable you to find evidence-based recommendations for baseline prevention. These solutions were designed with privacy in mind, offering a full lifecycle of security and protection for your personal information.

 

What type of information we collect

 

When you use our services, you may be asked to provide certain information, such as gender and age, that allows us to provide you with an answer tailored to your preferences. We do not transfer this information or retain this information on our servers and we do not associate it with other information that may identify you as an individual. Your selection may be saved on a cookie placed on your browser to recognize your options when you visit our website again.

 

Our website only collects aggregated data information on the topics and links that our users click on. We do not collect or have access to any personal information. You have the option to disable the use of cookies in your browser.

 

Where we store the information

 

We do not collect any personal information from you and have designed our solutions to collect minimum amount of information, none personal, to provide you with the answers you seek. This information you may be required to input is not identifiable and is not associated with any other information about you. In addition, this information is stored on a cookie that resides on your browser and we do not collect or store this information.

 

Your rights to your information

 

We do not collect or store any personal information about you so we would not be able to respond to a request for access to, deletion of, or correction of any personal information about you.
